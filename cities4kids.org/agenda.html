 1605-1735 hrs Session-II: Plenary Session on Urban95 The session Urban95 asks city leaders, urban planners, architects and engineers the question: if you could see the city from an elevation of 95 cm- the average height of a healthy 3-year-old what would you do differently? It is premised on the belief that if we want to make a city liveable for everyone, planning from the vantage point of a toddler is the best place to start.
